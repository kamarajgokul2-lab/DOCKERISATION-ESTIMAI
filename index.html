How the model works

Training Data

Trained on thousands of real residential property transactions, capturing price signals across area, amenities, location, and furnishing level.

Feature Engineering

Categorical fields like furnishing status are encoded numerically. Binary amenity flags let the model capture multiplicative effects on price.

Instant Inference

The serialised .pkl model is loaded once at server startup, keeping every prediction under a second — even at scale.
